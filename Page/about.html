About Us

Welcome to Asia Yummy! Our mission is to bring authentic flavors from Asia right to your plate.

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Phnom Penh, we aim to bring a unique dining experience that showcases the best of Asian cuisine. Every dish is crafted with passion and dedication to quality, ensuring a memorable meal every time.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the freshest ingredients, sourced from trusted suppliers.
  • Authenticity: Our recipes are rooted in traditional Asian culinary practices.
  • Community: We love being part of the Phnom Penh community and aim to give back in every way we can.
